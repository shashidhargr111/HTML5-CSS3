7.FORMAT FACTORY


  • FormatFactory is an ad-supported freeware multimedia converter that can convert video, audio, and picture files.
  • It is also capable of ripping DVDs and CDs to other file formats, as well as creating & iso images.
  • It can also join multiple video files together into one.
  • FormatFactory supports the following formats:
  1. Video formats
  2. Audio formats
  3. picture formats
